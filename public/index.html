Usage: take an organism that you want to identify. In the left field labeled "Taxon", search and select the Latin name of the group of species that you know it is part of. In the right field, paste coordinates (formatted like: "0.000,0.000") to the location where you found the organism. This proof-of-concept will attempt to find the best identification resource for your case.
